FAQ

What is this?

Codidact is a community-driven, non-profit-focused network of knowledge sharing websites. Our guiding principle is "For the community, by the community". See our project homepage for more information.

Do you have an idea for a community?

Do you have a community that is in need of Q&A software? If you can bring a core group of users, we'll host a site for you. Please suggest it on our Meta site by posting in the Site Suggestions category.

How can I contribute?

Anyone can contribute to Q&A on any of our sites: Create an account and you're good to go!
If you want to help with the code, we're currently developing in Ruby on Rails, which is what these sites are running on right now. If you want to contribute, you can visit us on GitHub.

I still have questions!

Check out the Codidact project homepage for an overview of what we're doing. You can also look around our Meta site to see if your question has been answered there, or ask a new question there.
